VASE P.A.100 AMPLIFIER

Links provided to schematics, photos and info doc for this 6 microphone input PA amplifier built by VASE circa late 60's, early 70's.

The amp has an inscription inside the cabinet with KEITH N KING and FERNTREE GULLY and JULY 1964. The phase inverter and output stage are almost an exact copy of the Dynaco Mk3 amplifier.
